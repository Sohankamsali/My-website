Outer banks

I visited in sep 2023

Outer banks is a best tourist attraction in tidewater region. It is a island surrounded by water. The beach in the evening looks so amazing and the sky changes color into purple. The beach is so beautiful. There is only one way to go there, a road fully surrounded by water. Fishing is the most popular tourism in outer tanks.
